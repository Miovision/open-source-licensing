Packaging and keeping the package current is not simple, for details see
README.Debian. We consider http://netpbm.alioth.debian.org/ the
technical upstream, and this release can be exported out of alioth by
cvs -z3 -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.alioth.debian.org:/cvsroot/netpbm export -r debian-9-25-2 netpbm
